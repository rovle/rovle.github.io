A Catalogue of Interspecies Friendships

It comes as a comfort to me when I see attachment between two species which aren't wired for connection between each other. It makes me feel that more is possible.

That's why I tried to create a non-exhaustive catalogue of various such friendships of which one can find videos on the internet.

To navigate this page, use the keyboard arrows or use the controls in the bottom right, or swipe right/left/down on mobile (additional pictures of animal pairs are often found "down"; if you are on PC you can use "Space" to go through everything).

Kumbali & Kago

Kumbali, the cheetah, needed a companion. After he got to know Kago, the two became inseparable.

Source video.

Philosophical interlude

(It should be noted that I do not consider these examples as a proof of some kind of a deep cosmic pattern of love between all beings. Indeed, I think that one can have any combination of "capability" and morality/empathy/goals in a being.

However, it might be evidence of the shared empathy backbone between mammals.

And that is nice, too, kinda.)
