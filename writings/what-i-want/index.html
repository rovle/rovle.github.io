What I want from you

Suppose you were visited by an omniscient being who told you that, if you were to expend your greatest efforts – truly your greatest efforts – humanity would survive and thrive.

Suppose it also told you that, if you were to fail to act, humanity would be extinguished – or worse.

Imagine in yourself the state of mind that you would be in after hearing such a proclamation. Imagine, too, the lengths to which you would then go. Imagine the urgency of your thoughts, the resoluteness of your actions.

That is how I want you to act.
